Zeta Beta Tau Fraternity

Delta Lambda Chapter

ABOUT US

ZBT Delta Lambda is a proud member of the Greek Life of Monmouth College in Monmouth, IL. We are an organization that strives to best exemplify our core values such as brotherhood and service. While we have lived in a variety of locations throughout our time at the college, we are currently housed in the B Wing of the college's fraternity complex: on the corner of 9th Street and Euclid Avenue. A small but mighty chapter of just over 20 members, ZBT Delta Lamba is known on campus to house The Best Boys in Town, and have always made apparent the difference between a "frat" and a "fraternity."

PHILANTHROPY

Children's Miracle Network

Children's Miracle Network Hospitals has been ZBT's national charity since 2002. All of our philanthropy events benefit the organization, with our biggest events being Get on The Ball and our annual Pancake Dinner.

Get on the Ball!

Once or twice a year, Delta Lambda hosts the Get on the Ball event where we roll a giant beach ball around campus. Students, faculty, and campus organizations can donate to the CMN and sign their name on the ball.

Pancake Dinner

Every spring, ZBT Delta Lamda hosts its annual Pancake Dinner. The event started in 2013 when a brother needed help raising funds for his mother's cancer treatment. Since her unfortunate passing, we remember her by hosting the event every year in memory of Michelle Forrester. The event now serves to raise funds for the American Cancer Society through donations, food, and raffles.
